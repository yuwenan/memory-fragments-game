# 「记忆碎片」— GPT 图片生成提示词

> 💡 使用方法：直接复制每张图的**提示词**到 GPT 对话框，让 GPT 用 DALL-E 生图即可。
> 每张图都带风格统一描述，确保整体画面一致。

---

## 🎬 封面/标题画面（游戏第一印象！）

**用途**：玩家打开游戏后看到的第一个画面——全屏封面，上面叠加标题和"开始游戏"按钮。这张图决定游戏的档次感。

**尺寸**：横版（landscape），宽屏比例

**提示词（复制这段给 GPT）**：

```
Create a cinematic dark title screen image for a mystery puzzle escape game. The scene shows a long abandoned research facility corridor viewed from eye-level, looking forward into deep darkness. A single flickering amber light source at the far end casts long dramatic shadows and illuminates countless dust particles floating in the air like frozen moments. Deep teal-blue and near-black shadows dominate the foreground and sides, fading to warm amber glow in the center distance. Shattered glass fragments on the floor catch and reflect the light in scattered bright points. Faint translucent hexagonal memory fragment shapes float subtly across the composition - each with soft purple-blue inner glow, as if digital ghosts. On the left side, a heavy metal door stands slightly ajar revealing absolute blackness beyond. On the right, peeling wall panels expose rusted pipes behind them. Atmospheric film grain texture throughout. Moody, mysterious, premium AAA game title screen quality. Dark atmospheric hand-drawn illustration style with rich textures and brush strokes. No text, no words, no UI elements. The bottom 35% should be darker/gradier to allow text overlay space.
```

**文件名**：`title_screen.png`
**存放路径**：`MemoryFragments/assets/backgrounds/`

---

## 🎨 通用风格关键词（已内嵌在每条提示词中，不用额外加）

- 暗色氛围 + 手绘质感
- 琥珀暖光（灯/屏幕发出的光）+ 深青阴影（角落/暗区）
- 2.5D 微透视（有轻微纵深感，不是完全平面）
- 废弃研究所设定——灰尘、旧金属、混凝土墙
- 无人物出现
- 高质量游戏素材感

## 🔄 如果图片不满意怎么办？

- **太暗了** → "Make the amber light brighter"
- **太亮了** → "Deeper shadows, darker overall"
- **太3D了** → "More flat illustration-like, less 3D rendering"
- **太简单了** → "Add more details - dust, scratches, wear marks"
- **风格不统一** → "Match previous image's exact style and color palette"
- **不需要文字** → "No text or words in the image itself"
